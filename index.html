Yoga Online & Offline Courses

We’ve collaborated with over 500 yoga instructors, wellness brands, and health-focused communities worldwide creating a diverse, inclusive, and mindful digital space for yoga lovers of all levels.

Private Yoga Sessions

Perfect for beginners, advanced practitioners, or those with specific physical needs. Whether you want stress relief, strength building, flexibility improvement, or deep relaxation, this class is adapted to you. Each session is designed to align with your personal goals—be it improving posture, enhancing mindfulness, or building overall body awareness.
You’ll receive individualized attention to ensure proper alignment, safe modifications, and gradual progress at your own pace. Our approach blends traditional yoga techniques with mindful breathing and guided meditation, creating a well-rounded experience for both body and mind.

Online And Visual Classses

Accessible, convenient, and designed for all levels, our online and virtual classes bring the yoga studio to you. Whether you’re at home, traveling, or balancing a busy schedule, these sessions make it easy to stay consistent with your practice.
Each class is live-streamed or pre-recorded, allowing you to choose the format that works best for you. Enjoy interactive guidance during live sessions or practice at your own pace with on-demand videos. All classes are led by experienced instructors who ensure you still receive proper cues and safe modifications, even from afar. Stay connected, stay committed, and experience the benefits of yoga wherever.

Group Yoga Classes

Experience the energy and connection of practicing yoga in a supportive group setting. These sessions are designed to bring people together—whether you’re a beginner seeking guidance or an experienced yogi looking to grow your practice.
Our group classes combine dynamic movement, breathwork, and mindfulness, helping you build strength, increase flexibility, and find inner calm. With a variety of styles and levels available, you’ll always find a class that fits your needs and schedule. Practicing alongside others not only keeps you motivated but also fosters a sense of community and shared growth. Each session is led by a certified instructor.

Frequently Asked Questions

where are you located and when is your opening hours

We are located at 123 Fitness St, Wellness City. Our opening hours are Monday to Friday from 6 AM to 10 PM, and Saturday to Sunday from 8 AM to 8 PM.

What types of fitness classes do you offer?

We offer a variety of fitness classes including yoga, pilates, HIIT, strength training, cardio kickboxing, and dance fitness. We also have specialized classes for beginners and advanced practitioners.

Do you offer personal training sessions?

Yes, we offer personal training sessions tailored to your individual fitness goals. Our certified trainers will work with you to create a customized workout plan and provide one-on-one coaching.

What membership options are available?

We offer various membership options including monthly, quarterly, and annual plans. We also have family and student discounts available. Please contact our front desk for more details on pricing and packages.

Do you provide nutrition and diet advice?

Yes, we have certified nutritionists on staff who can provide personalized diet plans and nutrition advice to complement your fitness routine. We also offer group nutrition workshops and seminars.

Are there any age restrictions for joining the gym?

We welcome members of all ages. However, individuals under the age of 16 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian while using the facilities. We also offer senior fitness programs tailored to older adults.

What safety measures are in place at your facility?

We prioritize the safety and well-being of our members. Our facility is regularly cleaned and sanitized, and we follow all local health guidelines. We also have trained staff on-site to assist with any emergencies or injuries.

Can I try a class before committing to a membership?

Yes, we offer a free trial class for new members. This allows you to experience our facilities and classes before making a commitment. Please contact us to schedule your trial session.
